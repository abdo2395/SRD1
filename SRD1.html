Introduction

The prototype is designed to measure different variables that are affected by climate change: temperature, humidity, soil moisture, and rainfall. After ensuring that the prototype met the design requirements, here comes the role of the prediction using a mobile application. The sensors are connected to the app via the Wi-Fi module to display the readings, being provided with the equations from the predictive model. Thus, if any measurement of the three measurements is measured, the other two will be predicted; thus, achieving its purpose. Once determined, this will show the threat to the agriculture and how negatively the change in the climate will affect the agriculture and the crop yield..
